This programme also provides the opportunity to develop as an entrepreneur in the field of fashion products manufacturing or marketing.

In the further higher study you can opt for MBA or Post Gradate Diploma in Apparel Design/Product/Fashion Accessory Design & Management. You can also take admission in Fashion Marketing/Fashion Technology Programme of National Institute of Design (NID) or at National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT). You have also the liberty to pursue your education in the other branches as per your likening i.e. International Business, Retail etc. This degree is valid for any competitive examination also. You are also eligible for CAT/XAT/MAT like management entrance examination.

Candidates having secured an aggregate of 50% in any stream from JAC/ICSE/CBSE or any other recognized board are eligible. The selection of the candidates will be made on the basis of the marks secured in the Entrance Test followed by Interview, to be notified on the college notice board/college website.
