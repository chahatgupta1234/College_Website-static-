Building Construction Management (BCM)

B.Voc Building Construction Management (BCM): This programme is also recognized by the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and Apparel, Made-ups & Home Furnishing Sector Skill Council. Apparel, Made-ups & Home Furnishing Sector Skill Council assesses the studentsâ€™ skills in following systems- After Completion of Semester-I After Completion of Semester-I+II After Completion of Semester-I+II+III+IV After Completion of Semester-I+II+III+IV Level-4 Level-5 Level-6 Level-7

So students will get dual certificate as Graduation Certificates from Ranchi University and Skill Assessment Certificate from Apparel, Made-ups & Home Furnishing Sector Skill Council. There are certain qualification packs designed by the Apparel, Made-ups & Home Furnishing Sector Skill Council for the every Level of Assessment. Students are assess in according to these qualification packs for ascertain their subjects understanding with skill components factors like practical and industry orientation. In this programme 240 hours of practical is allotted in each semester for the developments of skill components within the students in according to the guidelines issued by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

In the further higher study you can opt for MBA or Post Gradate Diploma in Marketing or Advertising Management. You have also the liberty to pursue your education in the other branches as per your likening i.e. International Business, Retail etc. This degree is valid for any competitive examination also. You are also eligible for CAT/XAT/MAT like management entrance examination.
