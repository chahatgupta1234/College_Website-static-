B.Comm in Accounts

This programme is designed to fulfill the demand of finance and auditing. Different areas are recognized in the finance i.e. risk management, export finance, investment,auditing, currency market operation, international money transactions etc. Accounts and finance has extreme vast areas of operation and presently gaining importance as the developments of business. This area also covers joint ventures, business mergers and corporate acquisition. Due to the present government initiative of make in India programme this field is also gaining importance. Another major area of finance is auditing and this is the mandatory requirement for every financial dealings. The demand for the auditors is growing day by day. This programme is also call as next generation programme and vast opportunities are exist in these fields.

The selection of the candidates will be made on the basis of the marks secured in the Entrance Test followed by Interview, to be notified on the college notice board/college website.
